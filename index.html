About us

Empowering Through Business Excellence

Dodom & Son (Pty) Ltd, founded in 2013 by the visionary Mabuza Nontsikelelo, is a proudly Black Economic Empowerment (BBBEE) company dedicated to delivering quality services and uplifting communities. Specializing in the supply of ICT and industrial equipment, stationery, computer peripherals, and consumables, we are committed to economic empowerment and poverty alleviation.

Our mission extends beyond business—we aim to create opportunities for women, youth, and disabled individuals while forming strategic partnerships and actively participating in business growth initiatives.
